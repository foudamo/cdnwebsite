How do we bring our interdisciplinary approach to our work?

  • DESIGN THINKING

  • We use the design thinking process to resolve social innovation challenges and bring together relevant stakeholders to develop successful innovative solutions. We offer education to start ups and the community in support of the above activities.

  • PLAY

  • We use play as intricate part of our design process. Innovation is a result of playful thinking, an approach that enables designers to bring together a number of different, unique ideas together to develop design offerings. To be creative it is vital to be able to play with ideas, imagine the future, innovation is a result of playful thinking.

  • VISUALIZATION

  • We reinforce the design process with visualizing techniques, such as experience mapping, that allow us to map out the opportunities and possibilities for our clients. Early visualization enables, exploration of possibilities, analysis of the issues, prototype alternatives, clarify direction and early client agreement.

  • PROCESS

  • We initiate our projects with an agreed upon project outline, that includes a design brief, timing, responsibilities and expectations. This process often identifies unexpected challenges and possible work arounds to achieve an optimum solution.
