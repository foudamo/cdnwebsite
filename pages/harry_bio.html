• Harry Mahler

  • FOUNDER | PRESIDENT

  • Harry Mahler is a strategic innovator, designer, and educator. He is an internationally recognized designer, having worked in staff positions and consultant designer roles on projects in Canada, Europe, and Asia.

  • With a keen interest in design innovation, Harry has worked on a broad range of projects, such as corporate identity, product branding, and product design for toys, medical equipment, and many more. He has also led design research projects for SickKids, CMHA, Toronto Hydro, and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. Harry has worked both as a professional designer and as a professor at McMaster University and the Ontario College of Art & Design. Moreover, he has contributed to the design community through his work as the president of the Association of Chartered Industrial Designers of Ontario. He has been elected as an Academician of the Royal Canadian Academy of Arts (RCA) for his contribution to the development of visual arts within Canada.
