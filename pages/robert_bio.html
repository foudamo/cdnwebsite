• Robert Fleisig

  • VP Of Research

  • Robert Fleisig is responsible for the growth of strategic partnerships with researchers both at universities and in healthcare institutions.

  • His mission is to leverage researcher expertise and scientific knowledge in the collaborative design of new products, services, and systems. Following his graduate work, Robert started his career working a dotcom startup. His industry experience includes a sabbatical at General Motors Canada’s Advanced Technology Group in Oshawa, Ontario. His recent clients have included Hatch, Union Gas, the City of Hamilton, and McMaster’s Children’s Hospital. Robert holds Bachelor’s, Master’s, and Doctoral degrees in Mechanical Engineering. He is a registered Professional Engineer in the Province of Ontario. He is a permanent Assistant Professor in the Walter G. Booth School of Engineering Practice at McMaster University and leads Innovation Studio for the Walter G. Booth School of Engineering at McMaster University. In 2014, he as awarded the President’s Award for Outstanding Contributions to Teaching and Learning.
